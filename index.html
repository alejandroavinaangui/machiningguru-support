How MachiningGuru Works

MachiningGuru helps users calculate CNC speeds and feeds, save machining setups with notes, scan G-code from photos stored on their phone, and preview supported toolpaths in an interactive 3D plotter.

G-Code Photo Scanning

MachiningGuru can scan visible G-code from a photo selected from your device. Recognition results may vary depending on lighting, image quality, text size, formatting, and clarity. Always review scanned code before using the toolpath preview.

Safety notice: MachiningGuru is intended as a calculation, documentation, and visualization aid. Always verify calculations, machine limits, tooling, offsets, and CNC programs before operating machinery.
